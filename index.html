Welcome to Leova's Trust Center, where our commitment to data privacy and security is central to our operations. Leova is a clinical trial database configuration system that takes a protocol and automates the generation of database configuration, including schedule of procedures, schedule of events, eCRFs, and edit checks.

This Trust Center offers detailed insights into our security practices, including the specific controls and policies implemented by our teams. You can explore our compliance standards, request access to comprehensive security documentation, and gain a clear understanding of how we safeguard your clinical trial data.

Additionally, we invite you to subscribe for updates to stay informed about the latest advancements in our security and compliance initiatives.
