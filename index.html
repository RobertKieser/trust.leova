Welcome to Leova's Trust Center, where our commitment to data privacy and security is central to our operations. Leova provides AI-powered clinical trial database configuration software that automates EDC setup from protocols, enabling clinical research teams to accelerate study launches while maintaining the highest standards of data integrity and regulatory compliance.

This Trust Center offers detailed insights into our security practices, including the specific controls and policies implemented by our teams. You can explore our compliance standards, request access to comprehensive security documentation, and gain a clear understanding of how we safeguard your data.
